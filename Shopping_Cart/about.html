Purpose

A shopping cart on an online retailer's site is software that makes it easy to purchase a product or service. Accepts customer payment and arranges for distribution of that information to merchant, payment processor, and other parties.

Audience

Users who dropped out of a funnel, abandoned items in their shopping carts, searched the site but did not convert, viewed any page in a directory, viewed a specific page, have converted and High-value users.
